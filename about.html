About iMaster Online Academy

At iMaster Online Academy, we believe in student-centered teaching and tailor our lessons to meet individual student requirements and absorption rates. Our vision is to transform education through innovative online learning experiences that empower students globally. We strive to be a pioneer in online education, providing accessible and high-quality learning opportunities that transcend geographical boundaries.

VISION

To lead the way in global education by providing personalized, high-quality online learning experiences that empower students worldwide.

MISSION

To deliver comprehensive, curriculum-aligned education through an engaging online platform, fostering critical thinking and preparing students for success in a connected world.

Meet Our Founder

Dr. Nusra Razideen

Driven by passion, innovation, and a deep commitment to educational excellence, Dr. Nusra Razideen founded iMaster Online Academy with one vision: to make quality education accessible to students worldwide. With a strong academic foundation from the Institute of Chemistry Ceylon- College of Chemical Sciences, and a teaching career that began in a private school, Dr. Nusra’s journey has been shaped by a desire to inspire and empower students.

She is currently pursuing a Master's degree at the prestigious University of Peradeniya, continuously expanding her expertise to deliver the best in online education.

Our Journey: Founded in late 2018, iMaster Online Academy started as a one-woman initiative with Dr. Nusra herself offering personalized tutoring. Since then, we’ve grown into a trusted name in online education, offering students innovative learning opportunities, expert guidance, and a commitment to excellence.
